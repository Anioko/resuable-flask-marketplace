Market Research Methodologies

Take a look at research strategies which we employ to collect, assemble and evaluate data. These strategies ensure that we provide valuable insights as outputs. These strategies are used on a case by case basis.

Face to Face Interviews

We initiate contact with research respondents face to face in order to conduct interviews with them based on questions asked by our clients.

Phone Interviews

This is a phone only based process where we contact respondents over the phone. We ask relevant questions, gather data and interprete the data.

Focus Groups

Depending on the type of demand, we gather focus groups, interview them and present the findings.

Online Surveys

We send out surveys via our online channels to respondents. We co-ordinate the process to ensure we reach minimum or maximum responses necessary.

Phone based - In depth Interviews

Some qualitative research questionnaires required more sessions with respondents, we take this approach on a case by case basis.

Face to face - In depth Interviews

We use in-depth face to face interviews for gathering qualitative research data. These decisions are made on a case by case basis.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are our answers to frequently asked questions. If you require information which is not available here, please contact us with such questions and we will provide you with answers.

  • How does it work?

    If you are interested in using our online service to conduct a market or product research, you create an account online, add questions and answer options, we take yur questions and contact respondents either face to face or via phone interviews or group sessions. We get answers, analyze them and send to you.

  • How many questions are we allowed to ask?

    Right now companies or organizations can only ask 10 questions per project. Unlimited projects can be created but there can only be 10 questions. One of the questions must be a screener question.

  • When are the results of the research survey available?

    Currently, we are unable to give a fixed duration for the return of data gathered. This is because of the manual processes involved. We endeavor to send back the results between 3 to 4 weeks of completed data gathering and data analysis.

  • What is a screener question and why do we need it?

    A screener is one question which you will set so that it can be used in deciding the segment or category or professions or groups of respondents which can provide answers that meet your criteria. The screener can be age, religion, employment status or location based.

  • Why do organizations have to register and create questions online?

    We are working to make the ordering process seemless for International and Nigerian based clients. This means that we take orders online only. It also allows us to introduce future upgrades where those respondents login online to answer questions. We want to digitize the process end to end, this will enable us increase the size of the data gathered.

  • How many results are provided per project?

    Results range from minimum of 500 responses to a maximum of 2500 responses. In the future we may increase the number of respondents.

  • What are steps to take when conducting MarketResearch in Nigeria?

    Market research can be conducted by different types of organizations. It doesn't always have to be for profit organizations only. Here are high level steps involved:

    1. Define the research or business problem or what good looks like for your business case
    2. Develop hypothesis i.e an idea or explanation for something that is based on known facts but has not yet been proved
    3. Start your project with us. We will determine the best research methodology and the channels to gathering the right data
    4. We gather data
    5. We analyse the data using proper statistical tools and frameworks
    6. We interprete the data, sumbit it to your organization
    7. You draw a conclusion
